# Nathan's Nathan's Famous Hot Dog Eating Contest

Official website for the annual Nathan's Nathan's Famous Hot Dog Eating Contest held every July 4th in Baylake Pines, Virginia Beach.

## Live Site
https://swimnerdtim.github.io/nathans-nathans/

## About the Contest
- **When:** Every July 4th at 12:00 PM
- **Where:** Sea Chest & Trelawney Roads, Baylake Pines, VA
- **Duration:** 3 minutes
- **Prizes:** Men's Mustard Belt, Women's Pink Pepto-Bismol Belt

## Current Champions (2025)
- **Men's:** Jimmy the Shark (10 dogs & buns - WORLD RECORD)
- **Women's:** Sam the Sausage Slayer & Jillian Batchelor (tie, 4 dogs)

## Features
- Live countdown to next contest
- Hall of Champions with records and stats
- Complete contest history (2019-2025)
- Official rules and regulations
- Photo gallery from past years
- Online registration
